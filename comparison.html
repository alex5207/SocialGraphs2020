Sentiment through the decades

Sentiment analysis (see below) on the movie descriptions and wikipedia pages reveals a very steady curve over time (notice the scale).

Even though the sentiment diverts in the 2010s, the average sentiment of the movie descriptions and wikipedia pages are so close that we cannot tell if there is a difference.
The right plot below shows the most positive and negative words used on the Wikipedia pages of each decade. Notice that most of the words are present in all decades. It seems like most of the words are needed to describe every decade

What is sentiment analysis?

Sentiment analysis is a technique to analyse the sentiment of a piece of text by analysing the hapiness of the words and tokens that comprise the text.

The average sentiment of movie descriptions and wikipedia pages does not reveal any changes in sentiment over time. Maybe the good is averaged out by the bad? After all, a whole lot happens in a decade and it can't be all that bad
