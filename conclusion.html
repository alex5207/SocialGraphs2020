Conclusions

This project has revealed some interesting facts about the network of Hollywood actors and actresses. Wee saw that the average actor has worked with 65 other actors and some (e.g Bruce Willis) have worked with more than 150 out of the 500 actors - Indeed a small world...
We also saw how the genres have developed over time, with Drama being the uncrowned Queen of Hollywood at all times.

On text analysis
Lastly, we attempted to analyse the movie description of Hollywood. It is clearly difficult to convey meaning to the words, but we can conclude that the type of language in movie descriptions is vastly different from descriptions in Wikipedia pages.

For future work, we recommend refining the language processing of the movie descriptions and reconsider whether to use Wikipedia pages as the sole truth. We genuinely believe that Hollywood is a product of the world we live in - Help us show it!
